Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy is a priority for SalesMeetings.AI LLC. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

We collect personal and non-personal information to provide and improve our services. This may include:

  • Personal details such as your name, email address, phone number, and company information.
  • Usage data, including IP addresses, browser types, and interaction with our website.
  • Cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and personalize our services.
  • To communicate with you, including updates, promotions, and service notifications.
  • To analyze user behavior and improve website functionality.
  • To comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Your data may be shared with trusted third parties solely for the purposes outlined in this policy, such as:

  • Service providers assisting in delivering our services.
  • Legal or regulatory authorities, as required by law.
  • Business partners, only with your consent.

Your Rights

You have the following rights concerning your personal data:

  • Access your data and request corrections.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt-out of receiving marketing communications.
  • Restrict or object to data processing in specific circumstances.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

SalesMeetings.AI LLC reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be communicated through our website, and continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
